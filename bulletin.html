6 月份教室上課時段 June Class Schedule

溫馨提示 Payment Notice

如欲於7月轉換教室或停學，須於6月20日或以前通知教室及填寫休會表格。

Students who want to change their schedule or pause in July must notify us and fill out the suspension form on or before June 20th.

5月31日前尚未辦理銀行自動轉帳者，請儘快向教室取各銀行自動轉帳授權書，填寫完整資料後，交由本中心代辦手續即可。

Students who have not set up auto pay arrangements before May 31st must contact us as soon as possible. We will prepare the necessary documents on your behalf.

  • 上課時請自備文具(如鉛筆、擦膠等)，中文及英文 EFL/ERP 學生請帶配備耳筒的平板/電話，用作使用公文語音學習程式。 Prepare your own stationery (pencils, erasers, etc.) For Chinese, EFL or ERP students, bring a tablet/phone with headphones for the Kumon app.
  • 使用 KUMON CONNECT 請帶備平板電腦及專用觸控筆，並提前充滿電源。 For students using Kumon Connect, please make sure to bring a compatible tablet and stylus pen that fully charged.
  • 進入教室時請測量體溫清潔雙手。 Sterilize your hands thoroughly and do a temperature check before coming.
  • 學生須準時到教室上課，如需調動時間及請假，敬請提前通知。 Please come on time based on your scheduled time slot. If there is a need to change your schedule, or request for a leave, please contact us in advance.
